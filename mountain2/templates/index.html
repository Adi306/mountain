About the test

The MBTI test is an assessment that identifies one’s 4-letter personality type, with each letter corresponding to a specific personality preference or tendency.
At its core, the theory behind the MBTI test is based on the fact that the world’s population is made up of these 16 different types of people—hence the 16 MBTI Personality Types.
